🎶 Prosody

Prosody isn’t just how something is said—it’s the timing, tone, and trust built into cadence. It lives in the delay of a gospel moan, in the silence before a beat drops.

“Prosody must be earned. It passes through your body first.”

Feel is a boundary, not of knowledge, but of experience. To cross it, you must listen longer than you speak.

🚪 Boundaries

Boundaries define. They constrain and liberate, exclude and clarify. Natural boundaries like rivers and synapses; human boundaries like laws and borders; artificial ones like copyrights and algorithms.

Intellectual property is a codified form of boundary—a modern shibboleth drawn in legalese instead of dialect.

“Shibboleths expose more than skill. They reveal origin, immersion, identity.”
